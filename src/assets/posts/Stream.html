I worked as a fulltime software developer for STREAM ticketing www.streamticketing.co.nz over the summer of 2010-2011. STREAM Ticketing is a Hamilton based, event ticketing and event access management company. I was a part of a small agile team working on a significant upgrade of their e-commerce website and back office system. In addition to developing new functionality in their legacy PHP based ticketing website, I was responsible for the design and implementation of a complex user flow for seat selection for multi event ticket packages.

The workflow for purchasing multi event passes and UI is my design. This was built into the companies existing site. The colour scheme and branding was pre-existing.
